An Artist has the power to make the portrait of any situation, and also has the ability to spread colors. Yes, Life is all about colors. Many customs and traditions around the world were emphasized on colors. They portrait each situations through colors. For instance, white for peace and black for misery. The custom and traditions illustrate black as an emptiness. So we, the "BlackPaperStudios" try to renovate it by spreading love and happiness through our creative ideas.
